Settings

Instrument & tuning

Every chord chart, diagram and the tuner follow this. Switching reloads the app.

Fretboard diagrams

Dots and finger-position numbers while you're learning new shapes, or clean patterns once you're moving by feel. With Patterns on, the maximized chord view names each shape.

Guidance level

Grades the one-time tips and how chord charts render. Advanced collapses chord grids to compact chips.

Theme

Auto follows your device's light/dark setting. Light and Dark stay put.

Accent color

Everything below lives only on this device. Back up saves it to a file. Drop that file in Drive or Files. On a new phone, Restore reads the file and brings everything across. Restore adds to and overwrites these items. It never deletes anything already here.
